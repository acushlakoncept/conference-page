DEF CON UYO - NIGERIA 2020

DEF CON brings Cyber Security experts from different facets together to combat both the present and future cyberg threats facing our industries.

The 2 day event will bridge the gap between the leading security solution providers and security professionals by bringing them onto the same pedestal. DEF CON UYO - NIGERIA 2020 will provide a variety of learning formats like Keynote presentations, panel discussions, fireside chats and exclusive networking sessions that will lead to meaningful business connections throughout Africa.

DEF CON Uyo - Nigeria 2020 Hackathon

This will be the First Penetration Testing Blackbox with AI based competition which is going to happen in the South South Zone of the Country. Cyber Security threats are becoming a thing of concern as more and more people go online as a result of the pandemic. In the past there has been several competitions but none of them has ever combined with Artificial Intelligence, which is why we are organising this competition. This will also encourage the students to think out of the box and get hands on experience on trending AI and Cyber Security Skills.
